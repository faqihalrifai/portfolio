My Workflow / Process

My workflow is a blend of technical and strategic processes, ensuring every project is not just a digital product, but a business solution.

1. Discovery & Research

Analyzing client needs, conducting in-depth market research, and identifying core problems to formulate clear and measurable project goals.

2. Planning & Wireframing

Developing project structure through wireframes and sitemaps, organizing information architecture, and planning key functionalities for optimal user experience.

3. UI/UX Design

Creating intuitive and visually appealing user interfaces, focusing on user experience (UX) through mockups, interactive prototypes, and usability testing.

4. Development

Implementing designs into functional and clean code, building core features, and ensuring seamless integration between front-end and back-end.

5. Testing & Iteration

Conducting thorough testing (functional, performance, security) to identify and fix bugs, and iterating based on user feedback and test results for product refinement.

6. Launch & Maintenance

Preparing and launching the product to the production environment, and providing ongoing support, updates, and performance monitoring to ensure long-term stability and relevance.
